Edit Skins

Swift Craft Launcher allows you to customize your player skin, switch between skin models, and manage capes.

Upload a Skin

  1. Click "Skin Manager" to open the skin management interface.
  2. The Skin Manager provides several options: "Switch Skin Model", "Upload Local Skin", "Cape Modification", and "Reset to Default Skin".
  3. Click "Upload Skin", select a local skin file from your computer, and click "Open".
  4. After confirming everything is correct, click "Apply Changes" to save your new skin.
  5. If the character avatar in the lower left corner is replaced with your new skin, the upload was successful.
