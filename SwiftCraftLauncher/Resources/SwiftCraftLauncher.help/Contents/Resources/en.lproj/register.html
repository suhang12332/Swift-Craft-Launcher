Login Account

Swift Craft Launcher supports three main login methods: Offline Mode, Microsoft Official Login, and LittleSkin Login.

Offline Mode

No internet verification required. Enter any username to start the game. Only suitable for single-player mode or servers with online authentication disabled.

  1. Click "Add Player" in the launcher.
  2. Select the "Offline" option in the top right, enter your desired player name, and click "Create Account".
  3. When your player information appears in the bottom left of the launcher, the account has been added successfully.

Microsoft Login

Authenticate using your official Microsoft account. This is the only way to access all official Minecraft features. You must own a purchased copy of Minecraft to use this method.

  1. Click "Add Player" in the launcher.
  2. Select the "Microsoft" option in the top right and click "Start Login".
  3. Click to allow opening the browser, then follow the instructions on the webpage to log in to your Microsoft account and authorize Swift Craft Launcher.
  4. Return to the launcher and confirm the addition.
  5. When your player information appears in the bottom left of the launcher, the account has been added successfully.

LittleSkin Login

A third-party login method. Authenticate using an account provided by the LittleSkin skin site, commonly used for custom skins and capes. Only supports single-player mode and servers that support this login method.

Note: LittleSkin login is under development. Stay tuned for updates.
